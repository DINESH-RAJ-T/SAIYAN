VEGETA - PRINCE OF SAIYAN

THE REASON TO HATE GOKU

Vegeta, as the Prince of Saiyans, held a strong sense of pride and believed in the Saiyan warrior code, which emphasized strength and dominance.
He viewed Goku as a "common trash" Saiyan, a low-class warrior who should have been far weaker than him.
Vegeta was constantly humiliated by Goku's unexpected power increases, which made Vegeta feel inadequate and challenged his belief in his own superiority.
